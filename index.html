Who We Are & Why We Exist

Founder Patsy Arrechea

Hello, my name is Patsy Arrechea. I personally experienced domestic violence and homelessness with three children. I know firsthand how difficult it is when shelters are full.

Become of Light & Resilience provides survivors of domestic violence and their children with immediate support, safety, and hope. Through our Emergency Safe Nights Pilot, teen prevention workshops, and court advocacy programs, we ensure that families in crisis have a safe place to stay.

"As a kindergarten teacher in Boston Public Schools for 9 years, my mission is to provide families with immediate safety and hope."

The Problem

Shelters in Greater Boston are often at capacity. Without immediate support, survivors are at risk of further harm.

The Solution

Our Emergency Safe Nights Pilot partners with hotels to provide 1–3 night stays while long-term placement is arranged.
