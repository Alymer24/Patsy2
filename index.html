BEYOND SURVIVAL

Emergency Safe Nights Pilot

THE PILOT

When local shelters are full, survivors often have nowhere safe to stay. Our Emergency Safe Nights Fund provides temporary hotel accommodations for 1–3 nights, ensuring immediate safety while longer-term solutions are arranged.

YEAR 1-2 GOALS

  • 50-100 Emergency Hotel Stays
  • 10 Healthy Relationship Workshops
  • 20 Court Accompaniments

FOUNDER'S PLEDGE

Patsy Arrechea is personally seeding the first emergency nights to ensure the program is active and trackable from day one.
